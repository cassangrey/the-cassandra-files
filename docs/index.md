# The Cassandra Files

## Rationale

I created this site because I am concerned about what has happened to a disabled woman I have known for more than a decade, and about what has happened to those of us who tried to help her.

I did not begin as a campaigner or commentator. I became involved because I witnessed enough to believe that she needed support, that important evidence needed preserving, and that the version of events being repeated about her did not match what I had seen.

This site is a controlled briefing record for journalists, legal professionals, oversight bodies and relevant public-interest organisations. It is intended to let those readers examine the chronology, the evidence status and the institutional record for themselves.

It is not a public document dump, a social-media argument, or a request that anyone simply accept my interpretation of events.

## What this record concerns

The material concerns documented and reported issues around post-separation abuse, stalking, harassment, coercive control, serious sexual-offence allegations, safeguarding, disability access, police handling, court process and institutional decision-making.

The site does not present disputed allegations as proven facts. It distinguishes between direct witness account, documented record, reported allegation, public source material, restricted source material and unresolved questions.

The central question is:

**What did the relevant institutions know, what could they have checked, and why were the underlying concerns not properly investigated before serious action was taken against her?**

## How to read it

Start with **The record**. It is the main chronology. Each entry shows the date, the public-facing record, the evidence item, the evidence access position and the publication level.

Some entries include links to public or redacted anchor evidence. Other entries are summary-only, available on request, discussable as background, or legally restricted. Where detail cannot safely be shown, the table may still indicate that a dated record exists.

## Access limits

The table uses staged access levels:

- **Public**: the source is public or a redacted anchor document is linked directly.
- **Public summary only**: the record can be summarised, but the underlying evidence is not published here.
- **Available on request**: the material may be shown to a verified recipient where there is a clear professional or public-interest purpose.
- **Discussable on background**: the issue can be discussed as witness or context evidence, but the source itself should not be republished from this site.
- **Held; legally restricted**: the record exists, but the underlying material is not for publication here.

Protected court material, child-identifying information, private personal data, raw screenshots, unreviewed documents and material that could create legal or contact risk are not published here.

No information on this site should be treated as permission to contact any private individual involved in the underlying matters. Further evidence is shared only in stages, and only where the purpose, recipient and legal position justify it.
